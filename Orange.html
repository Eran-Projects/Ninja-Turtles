Orange Turtle - Michelangelo

Michelangelo, nicknamed Mike or Mikey, is usually depicted wearing a orange eye mask. Michelangelo is the most naturally gifted of the four brothers but prefers to have a good time rather than train. he is the youngest and most energetic of the team. His signature weapons are a single or dual nunchaku, though he has also been portrayed using other weapons, such as a grappling hook, manriki-gusari, kusarigama, tonfa, and a three-section staff.
